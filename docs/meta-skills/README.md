# Meta-Skills

Skills about how you use skills. These work with any tool (AI or human) and are behavior-level, not tool-specific.

## Available Meta-Skills

### [Session Debrief](session-debrief.md)
Extract durable learning at the end of any work session.

**Use when:** You finish a session and want to capture what you learned that will matter in future sessions.

**Output:** Structured takeaways with durability reasoning (`[TYPE] takeaway — why it's durable`).

---

### [Decision Protocol](decision-protocol.md)
A 5-stage protocol for architectural decisions: Inform → Compare → Critique → Connect → Commit/Pause.

**Use when:** You're about to make a choice that's expensive or hard to reverse.

**Output:** Logged decision in DECISIONS.md with explicit rationale and next-review checkpoint.

---

## How to Use These Files

1. **Before a session:** Review the relevant meta-skill's trigger question.
2. **During/after:** Follow the steps or format.
3. **Log findings:** Store takeaways in `inbox.md` (for captures) or `_core/learnings.md` (for archive).
4. **Connect:** Link related entries to `DECISIONS.md` or tag them for future discovery.

## Design Principle

These are **behavior-level** descriptions, not software-specific. They work in Terminal, on paper, in a meeting, or with any AI tool. The format is copy-paste ready. Adapt the trigger questions and storage locations to your workflow.
